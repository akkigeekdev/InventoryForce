ISAR Dr. Sadhana Desai Endowment fund

  1. The fund is set up with the intention of providing assistance to financially needy couples for undergoing IVF (In vitro fertilisation) treatment
  2. Contributions to the fund will be mainly from Dr. Sadhana Desai or her charitable trust. The contributions from Dr. Sadhana Desai may be in cash or kind.
  3. Contributions to the fund will also be accepted from other individuals or entities who wants to make contribute towards the cause for which the Fund has been established. Such contribution may also be in cash or in kind.
  4. Application from prospective beneficiaries should be made physically till such time the online application facility is set up, post which, the application has to be made online only.
  5. The applications should be made by the husband and the wife jointly. The required information should be for both the husband and the wife.
  6. The identity (aadhar), address and income proofs (income Tax Returns or Form 16), the applicants should be attached with the application.
  7. The committee overseeing the Fund may require the applicants to attach such other documents including medical reports with the application which the committee considers necessary for proper evaluation of the application.
  8. The application should be accompanied by a letter from the sponsoring ISAR member confirming the medical eligibility and genuineness of the applicants. It is the responsibility of the sponsoring ISAR member to verify the credentials of the applicants.
  9. The applicants should be heterosexual, married, Indian copies below 35 years of age. Marriage certificate should be attached to the application.
  10. The applicants should not be related to an ISAR member or IVF Centre in any way.
  11. The IVF treatment should be the first or the second to be carried out by the couple.
  12. The IVF treatment should be for the implanting of applicant's own gamete. No third party reproduction including surrogacy will be considered.
  13. The maximum amount of assistance for an IVF treatment will be Rs. 50,000/-(Fifty thousand only) per application.
  14. The assistance will be for only one IVF treatment of a couple.
  15. The doctor in charge of the IVF centre should be an ISAR member.
  16. The IVF Centre should be agreeable for inspection of the patient medical records and have protocols in place for maintainance and assessment of the records of the IVF procedure carried out.
  17. Applications for assistance from the Fund will be considered quarterly and preferably applications from only one zone will be considered for a particular quarter. The applications from that zone will be considered again only after the turn of all other zones are over.
  18. The assistance amount will be paid only after the completion of the IVF treatment and on receiving the report of the treatment from the IVF centre where the treatment is being carried out.
  19. The payment will be made directly to the IVF centre and the receipt will be issued by the centre in the name of 'ISAR Dr. Sadhana Desai Endowment Fund'.
  20. The applicants will have to indemnify ISAR for any act of omission or commission on the part of the IVF treatment centre including any treatment deficiency.
  21. Taxes including GST if any, applicable on the treatment will be the responsibility of the IVF Centre.
  22. The donations received in kind will be distributed based on the requirement of the beneficiaries.
  23. The decision of the committee as to the selection of the applicants, payment of assistance and distribution of assistance in kind will be final.
  24. The above terms and conditions may be modified by the committee overseeing the Fund at any time at their discretion.
